How to Extract Last Frame from MP4

This workflow is ideal for creators who only need the ending shot from an MP4 for continuity, thumbnails, or handoff into the next AI clip.

Step-by-step

  1. Open finalframe.video and upload your MP4.
  2. Wait for the final five timestamps to render.
  3. Click the frame you want to download.
  4. If your codec fails in-browser, server fallback will process it automatically.

Quality Tips

  • Use MP4 with H.264/AAC for best compatibility.
  • If you only need review frames, shorter clips upload faster.
  • For AI continuity, pick the cleanest frame from the last five, not always the final timestamp.
