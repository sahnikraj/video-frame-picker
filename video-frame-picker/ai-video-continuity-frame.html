AI Video Continuity: Use End Frame as Start Frame

In multi-shot AI workflows, continuity improves when you pass the final frame of clip A into clip B as a visual anchor.

Continuity Workflow

  1. Generate clip A and extract its last five frames.
  2. Pick the frame with the clearest composition and subject shape.
  3. Use that frame as image reference for clip B prompt.
  4. Repeat per shot to reduce jump cuts and style drift.

Prompting Tips

  • Keep camera direction and focal length terms consistent.
  • Use concrete subject anchors (wardrobe, lighting, scene elements).
  • Avoid large style shifts between chained clips.
